About Us

Licensed electricians who show up and get it right

CE Electric LLC started with one truck and a promise to do electrical work the way it's supposed to be done: to code, on schedule, no shortcuts. Fifteen years and a lot of job sites later, that promise hasn't changed.

Every electrician on our trucks is trained on current NEC code, background checked, and drug tested before they ever set foot on your property. We pull permits when the job requires it, and we don't leave a panel open overnight or a job half done.

We've wired new construction in Round Rock, upgraded panels in fifty-year-old homes in San Antonio, and run emergency service calls at 2am in Austin. Different jobs, same standard every time.
